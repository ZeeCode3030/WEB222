About the Author

My name is Hephzibah Nick-Oshomah, I am 20 years old and I hail from Nigeria. My hobbies include reading and drawing, the latter I would say is my talent.

The reason why I chose Scrabble is because It holds a special place in my heart as it was one of the first board games I learned as a child. My father studied literature in university, so he made sure I and my sisters were especially learned when it came to english. Scrabble was one of the tools he introduced to us to help us gain a wider vocabulary not to mention he absolutely loved playing it with us but when he couldn't, we settled for playing it on the family computer.

Needless to say, my father's plan worked and even got me interested in reading literature. I would fully recommend this game to anyone, young or old because you never stop learning and it is so much fun
